Step 1. Extract zip at any place you want.
Step 2. Open the extracted folder and get into 'patient' folder
Step 3. Open terminal(cmd/powershell) in the 'patient' folder
Step 4. Hit the command 'docker-compose build'
Step 5. On complition of Step 4 hit command 'docker-compose up'
Step 6. Open the latest pgAdmin4 (>=v6)
Step 7. connect to the server on localhost with port 5440 and username=postgres and password=@pass1234
